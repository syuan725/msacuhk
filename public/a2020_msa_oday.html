Malaysian Orientation Day 2020/21

6 Sep 2020

Although the 1st term of the academic year 2020/21 was conducted online, we hoped that freshmen could gain some understanding of CUHK and Hong Kong through this event.

We also aimed to improve the bonding between freshmen and to establish a close relationship between freshmen and CUHK Malaysian seniors.

As this event was held virtually on Zoom, freshmen were able to experience online learning through playing games and utilising the functions in Zoom’s meeting room.
