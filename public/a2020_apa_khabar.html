Apa Khabar?

8 Nov 2020

“Apa Khabar?” literally means “How are you?” in English. This virtual gathering was aiming to provide a platform for members to catch up with each other after the mid-term period, and to foster relationships and better understanding among members.

Three breakout rooms were created in Zoom’s meeting room to carry out different activities, namely “Among Us Room”, “Karaoke Room”, and “Interactive Games Room”. Attendees could stay in the main room to chit chat with others as well. Participants were granted access to “move” between each room freely, making it more interesting as if they were joining a physical event.
