Family Tree System

Established in the academic year 2019/20, this system is aiming to help Malaysian freshmen kick-start their university life in CUHK. This parenting system will match freshmen with CUHK Malaysian seniors according to their majors, personalities and interests. Freshmen (children) are welcomed to approach their seniors (parents) if they have any doubts before starting and during their studies in CUHK.
